Staining

Deck Depot offers staining for both fences and decks

Whether your fence is newly installed or a few years old, it can benefit from a quick and simple improvement: stain. Applying wood stain to your fence can improve its design and even its lifespan. Deck depot allows you to personally select your own color of either solid or semi-transparent stain.

Deck Depot has been staining decks and fences for decades, and would be proud to upgrade your fence at a moments notice. If you wish to receive more details, or get a free price estimate, please select one of the options listed on the right.

Staining instructions

Fence Staining Instructions

If you would like an estimate price for the staining of a fence, please follow these instructions. If the fence is shared, first confirm the willingness of the other parties to go through with the staining. Also make sure each party is in agreement about which color the fence will be stained in. Next, press the "Get an Estimate" button below, and fill out the form given, entering in the details of the project. We will get back to you as soon as we can.

Deck Staining Instructions

For deck staining, usually the process is a lot more straight forward. All you need to do is click the "Get an Estimate" button below and fill out the form given. If you have a job that does not fit any of the predefined options on the form, please mention that in the "Message box". We will get back to you as soon as we can.
